## Projects

### [Seismic Risk Assessment for Philippine Regions](https://github.com/angelpelausa/philippine-earthquakes-analysis)

**Objective:** Evaluate earthquake patterns and identify areas requiring priority attention for disaster preparedness.

**Approach:** Developed a custom data collection system to gather earthquake records from official sources. Processed and analyzed eight years of seismic data to track frequency changes, geographic distribution, and depth characteristics.

**Technical Execution:** Python for data extraction and processing, Power BI for interactive reporting

**Key Outcomes:**
- Documented a significant increase in seismic activity over recent years
- Pinpointed specific provinces with concentrated earthquake occurrences
- Established correlation between earthquake strength and depth
- Delivered insights to support regional safety planning

### [Healthcare Operations and Financial Analysis](https://github.com/angelpelausa/healthcare-data-analysis)

**Objective:** Examine patient care patterns and revenue cycle effectiveness for a hospital system.

**Approach:** Structured and analyzed patient encounter data to identify trends in visit types, insurance coverage, and treatment costs. Established automated reporting between database and visualization tools.

**Technical Execution:** SQL for data querying, Power BI for dashboard development

**Key Outcomes:**
- Quantified insurance coverage gaps affecting hospital revenue
- Tracked patient volume growth over a ten-year period
- Analyzed cost variations across different payment sources
- Provided data foundation for operational improvements

### [Global Supply Chain Performance Review](https://github.com/angelpelausa/supply-chain-analysis)

**Objective:** Assess operational efficiency and sales trends for an international logistics company.

**Approach:** Processed comprehensive supply chain data to evaluate delivery performance, customer distribution, and product category results. Created visualizations to communicate findings across business units.

**Technical Execution:** Python for data preparation, Tableau for interactive dashboards

**Key Outcomes:**
- Measured on-time delivery rates across shipping methods
- Identified significant sales decline periods requiring investigation
- Analyzed customer concentration risks
- Highlighted payment collection and fraud detection issues

### [Workforce Retention and Attrition Analysis](https://github.com/angelpelausa/hr-attrition-analysis)

**Objective:** Identify factors influencing employee turnover and recommend retention strategies.

**Approach:** Examined human resources data to understand departure patterns across departments, job roles, and employment tenure. Developed metrics to track turnover drivers and predict retention risks.

**Technical Execution:** Power BI for data modeling and visualization, Power Query for data transformation

**Key Outcomes:**
- Revealed department-specific turnover challenges
- Identified early-career retention opportunities
- Quantified impact of overtime on employee retention
- Supported HR strategy with data-driven insights

### [Retail Sales Performance Optimization](https://github.com/angelpelausa/coffee-shop-sales-analysis)

**Objective:** Analyze sales operations for a multi-location coffee business to identify improvement opportunities.

**Approach:** Processed transaction data to evaluate store performance, product sales patterns, and customer behavior across time periods. Created comprehensive sales reporting system.

**Technical Execution:** Excel for data analysis and dashboard creation

**Key Outcomes:**
- Compared performance metrics across store locations
- Analyzed product category sales and revenue contributions
- Identified seasonal and daily sales patterns
- Provided operational recommendations based on data findings
